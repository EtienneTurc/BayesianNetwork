BaNetOn

A graphical online tool to model and visualize bayesian networks

  • Overview

    This application is a simple and easy online tool to model and visualize a bayesian network.
    You can create your own bayesian network.
    Compute the probability of each node with regard to attributed nodes.
    Export and save your network to show your friends !

  • Get started

    Load a network by simply clicking on the Load button.
    Add nodes by double clicking.
    Draw edges by pressing shift and clicking on the nodes.
    Delete an edge or a node by pressing delete.
    Compute the probabilities by clicking the Compute button.
    Save your network by simply clicking on the Save button.

  • No nodes selected

    Select a node by double clicking on it.

  • Compute

    Click on the Compute button.
